Company's Success

Over the decades, DC has grow into one of the most popular and favourited franchises of all time. DC has had four of the top ten best selling comics of all time, and has sold over 30 million comic book copies. Although Marvel has had a larger market share value, DC has had four of the top ten best selling superhero comic series. The DC universe has a gross income of 5.5 billion dollars in the box office including the money earned from the many movies and TV series/shows.

DC Comics has had many great films and TV shows, but their top three best selling movies are The Wonder Woman with a lifetime gross amount of $412,563,408, The Aquaman with a lifetime gross of $355,061,807, and Batman Vs. Superman with a lifetime gross of $330,360,194.
