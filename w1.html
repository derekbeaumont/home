DC Comics' Goals and Background

|

Before, comic-book heroes such as Static, Icon, and Hardware created novel opportunities for minorities to be represented in popular super-hero comic books. The book that released their adventures now aims to include new creators and advertising opportunities.

The Milestone is an effort to find new writers and artists with a variety of backgrounds in the comic book industry, will be launched by WarnerMedia through its DC Comics section. The project is a tribute to Milestone Media, the now-iconic comic book line created in the early 1990s by a group of Black authors and published by DC. The financial services company Ally Financial, which focuses on digital platforms, will provide sponsorships and advertising support for the project.

"Superhero and comic culture is massive and has a broad reach, so I think we are going to get a really big universe of people that will really be into this", company Ally's chief marketing and public relations officer, Andrea Brimmer said when she was interviewed and told her opinion on the project. DC Comics is giving opportunites to a development program and a including a one-week-in training period in the Burbank, California DC Comics offices to American based writers and artists. The company's goal is to hire a group of writers, illustrators, and creative artists to help contribute to the publishing of the DC Comic book content.
